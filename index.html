About me

My Name is Ariel I currently live in Grand Junction, Colorado with my partner and single cat. I moved to Grand Junction from Steamboat Springs, Colorado were I grew up in 2019. I have an Associate of Arts degree with an emphasis on early childhood education from Colorado Mountain College. am currently enrolled in the Denver University coding boot camp for web development. Where I hope to become a full time web developer in the near future. And turn into a digital nomad. When I’m not in class, I enjoy reading, writing urban fiction in a collaborative setting and dabbling in graphic design. I also love to hike in the Colorado National Monument, and nearby areas, that surround Grand Junction. Fun Fact: I live near the Grand Mesa, which is the largest mesa in the world.
